PROJECT 2 : MY MAGIC MIRROR

PART 1 : SKETCH PHASE

Here is my sketch for my magic mirror:

  • On the top left on the screen we will have a weather midget with a max opacity to let the user see through it . This weather will be made using the weather API
  • on the top right I plan to have the clock time with precise design , to help the user know exactly what's the time , will also add a cool animation
  • On the bottom left we will have the news feed preview that will cycle. the user will have a link each time a news pop out , he can then decide to click on it to see or just look at the preview! the news will come form a JSON file
  • on the mid-right side of the mirror , the user will have a personalized calender with a list of things he has to do on the date that the calendar will display. The list will come from a JSON file
  • On the bottom right, the user will have a cool BPM feature. He would be able to see his heart beat in front of the mirror and his weight! Both BPM and weight will come from JSON file.
  • To finish the background is just an invert webcam to act exactly like a mirror
